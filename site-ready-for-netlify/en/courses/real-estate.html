Lesson 1: Buy or Rent?

üè† The Great Housing Decision

One of the biggest financial decisions young couples face is whether to buy or rent. This choice affects your lifestyle, wealth building, and financial flexibility for years.

‚úÖ Benefits of Buying

  • Build equity instead of paying landlord
  • Stable housing costs (fixed mortgage)
  • Freedom to renovate and customize
  • Potential appreciation over time
  • Tax benefits and deductions

‚úÖ Benefits of Renting

  • Lower upfront costs
  • More flexibility to move
  • No maintenance responsibilities
  • Can invest down payment elsewhere
  • No property tax or insurance costs

üìä The 1% Rule Test

A simple test: If monthly rent is less than 1% of home purchase price, renting might be better financially.

Example: $300,000 home √ó 1% = $3,000/month
If rent is $2,500/month ‚Üí Renting saves $500/month for investments

Lesson 6: Property Management & Building Wealth

üè† Maintaining Your Investment

üìÖ Regular Maintenance Schedule

  • Monthly: HVAC filters, check leaks
  • Quarterly: Gutter cleaning, smoke detectors
  • Annually: HVAC service, roof inspection
  • Bi-annually: Deep cleaning, exterior maintenance

üí∞ Budgeting for Maintenance

Set aside 1-3% of home value annually for maintenance and repairs

Example: $300,000 home = $3,000-9,000/year maintenance budget

Understanding Compound Interest

üöÄ The Power of Time

Starting early is the most powerful factor in building wealth. Even small amounts can grow significantly over time due to compound interest.

  • $500/month from age 25 ‚Üí $1.6M at 65
  • $500/month from age 35 ‚Üí $740K at 65
  • Starting 10 years later costs $860K!

üìà Rate of Return Matters

Small differences in return rates make huge differences over time. That's why choosing good investments is crucial.

  • 6% annual return ‚Üí $1.0M after 30 years
  • 8% annual return ‚Üí $1.5M after 30 years
  • 10% annual return ‚Üí $2.3M after 30 years

üí™ Consistency Pays Off

Regular monthly contributions are more important than timing the market. Dollar-cost averaging reduces risk.

  • Automatic investing removes emotion
  • Buy more shares when prices are low
  • Buy fewer shares when prices are high
